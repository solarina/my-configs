Obsidian - Rarely used it but its still a good app for notes taking and its all connected and fully anonymous unless you pay then you can use their cloud to backup and if not you can manually backup your file to proton drive as an example

Proton Ecosystem - This is a must, just dont let yourself have only 1 email account, have multiple because in today's day and age you don't know what may happen and overall their ecosystem is very good and well worth the money if you can afford it.

Ublock Origin - Best adblocker, do not use any others because they are all shit compared to ublock. I have a config in the repo so you can use it (lists are from celenity, they aren't my own and i don't take credit, i just use them and love them)

QBittorent - how many times i've seen people use UTorrent when in actuality they should be using this and it's not even a question

Signal Desktop - if you want to escape discord, this is your choice and overall its way better than any other app but it can get annoying sometimes with backuping and switching devices.
